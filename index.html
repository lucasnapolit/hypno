About HYPNOSIS

HYPNOSIS was born from a single conviction: that New Zealand deserved an underground electronic music platform built to international standards — uncompromising in sound, aesthetic, and intent.

Founded in Auckland in 2022, HYPNOSIS operates at the intersection of hard techno, acid techno, and hard groove — some of the most visceral and technically demanding forms of electronic music. More than an event promoter, it is a brand dedicated to shaping high-impact, immersive experiences that prioritize intensity, atmosphere, and connection.

Each event is approached as a controlled architectural experience — from soundsystem selection and room configuration to visual identity and crowd curation. HYPNOSIS is not about spectacle, but about depth, duration, and the raw energy that emerges when every element is aligned.

We are not a promoter in the conventional sense. We are curators of frequency and space. Every edition is conceived as a ritual — a disciplined process of immersion that strips away commercial noise and replaces it with intention.

With references rooted in Berlin's concrete floors and Detroit's industrial legacy, HYPNOSIS channels global underground culture while remaining deeply connected to New Zealand's evolving scene — shaped by its geography, isolation, and growing hunger for something real.

Operating across New Zealand, the brand is building a loyal community and a clear artistic identity, bridging international headliners with emerging local talent. HYPNOSIS continues to position itself as a driving force in the country's underground movement — raising the standard of what a rave can be.
